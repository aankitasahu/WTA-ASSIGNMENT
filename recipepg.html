THE BEST Cold Coffee

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making the best cold coffee at home! We all know how to make coffee but that cafe style cold coffee just on another level. Follow this simple recipe to have an awes way to start your day with a cup of cold coffee. Best part? You're gonna save a lot by not visiting starbucks every morning.

Ingredients

  • ICE (ofc)
  • Coffee (Instant or espresso anything that is available)
  • Milk
  • Sweetners (Optional)

Instructions

  1. If you're using instant coffee(I personally love the freeze dried ones) add sweetner as per your liking and add some water.
  2. Now, the secret to a boujee coffee is to froth it up. If you do not have a frother no worries, get a small jar and put the coffee, water, sweetner in it and shake shake shake.
